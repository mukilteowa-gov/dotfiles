## ‚ö° Quick Tips

- **Leader key**: `Space`
- **Exit insert**: `Esc` or `C-[`
- **Save**: `:w`
- **Quit**: `:q`
- **Save & quit**: `:wq`
- **Force quit**: `:q!`
